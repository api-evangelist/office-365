Microsoft announced the general availability of Work IQ API endpoints on June 16, positioning the platform as intelligence infrastructure designed specifically for AI agents. The service unifies chat, context, tools, and workspaces while maintaining secure, production-ready intelligence with built-in governance and permission-aware data access across Microsoft 365 and external systems. Work IQ operates independently of Microsoft 365 Copilot licensing on a consumption-based model, with new enterprise controls available through the Microsoft 365 admin center.
