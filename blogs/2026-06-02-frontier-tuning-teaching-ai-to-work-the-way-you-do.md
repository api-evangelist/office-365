Microsoft announced Frontier Tuning, a new capability that uses reinforcement learning to customize AI agents to match specific business processes and data within a managed compliance boundary. The technology enables organizations to tune models with their own workflows, terminology, and conventions without requiring data science expertise, and operates within compliance boundaries using proprietary data. Private preview is available via Forward Deployed Engineers, with future availability planned for Copilot Studio and Microsoft Foundry.
